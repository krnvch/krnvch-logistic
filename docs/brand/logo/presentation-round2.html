Alfredo's Take

Option A (Grid G) is the purest mark. One shape, one letter, no ambiguity. The [G]rida lockup is elegant — the mark IS the first letter. Cleanest at small sizes.

Option B (The Signal) is the most distinctive and abstract. The brackets say "precision" without spelling anything. Works beautifully as a standalone mark, but needs the wordmark for recognition.

Option C (Scoped G) merges both ideas. The brackets add a narrative layer ("looking through the viewfinder at G") but also add complexity. At 16px, the brackets are barely visible — so at favicon size, it effectively becomes Option A anyway.

My pick: Option A. The simplest mark is the strongest mark. The G alone is already geometric, already grid-like, already says "Grida." Adding brackets is adding decoration — and Grida doesn't decorate. But if you love the brackets, Option C works at larger sizes and degrades gracefully to just the G at small sizes.
